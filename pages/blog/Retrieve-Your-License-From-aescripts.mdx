## Retrieve License after Purchase when bought on aescripts.com

If you bought on <Link href={"https://aescripts.com"}>aescripts.com</Link>, a different reseller than Autokroma.com, you can retrieve your license in your user account :

1. Log in into <Link href={"https://aescripts.com/customer/account/login/"}>aescripts.com/customer/account/login/</Link>
2. Go to your account page
3. On the left, click on "My Downloads and Licenses"
4. Copy the license under "License Code"

<BlogImage
  src={require("assets/images/_BR/5/BRAW_Studio_getting_License_code.jpg")}
  hasShadow
/>

Now you can paste this license in the Settings popup of all Autokroma plugins !
